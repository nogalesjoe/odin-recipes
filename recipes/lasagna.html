World's Best Lasagna

Image by Schellenberg, licensed under CC BT-SA 4.0, via Wikimedia Commons.

Description

Lasagna is a classic Italian dish made with layers of pasta, meat sauce, cheese, and herbs

This recipe is simple enough for beginners and makes a delicious family meal.

Ingredients

  • Lasagna noodles
  • Ground beef
  • Marinara sauce
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Ricotta cheese
  • Parmesan cheese

Steps

  1. Cook the noodles.
  2. Brown the ground beef.
  3. Mix with the sauce.
  4. Layer noodles, meat sauce, and cheeses.
  5. Bake for about 45 minutes.
  6. Let cool before serving.
